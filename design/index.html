About Pycon Nigeria

The PyConNG 2017 is the largest annual gathering of Python geeks in Nigeria. It is organised by members of the Python Users Nigeria Group, a nonprofit organisation dedicated to advancing the Python community in Nigeria. Python developers get to learn, network and collaborate during the workshops, it’s a place to be!
