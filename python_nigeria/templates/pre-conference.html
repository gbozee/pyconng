Reach the Right Audience

An industry conference with a community spirit. More than half of our delegates are IT professionals, drawn from industry, research agencies, and government. We also attract a high number of University-level students, helping to shape the next generation of Python developers. Many of our speakers and delegates are contributors to key Open Source Python projects, used by millions of developers around the world.

Recruit Top technical Talent

Take advantage of the conference by having access to some of the best Pythonistas in the Industry all in the same room. PYCON is the place to connect with skilled Python developers looking for job opportunities. Sponsoring allows you to promote open roles and recruit talent.

Promote latest Products/Services

The exhibition hall allows sponsors to showcase new products, technologies, and services. Provide first access to new innovations at PyCon Nigeria. PyCoon receives coverage in technical publications, media, and more. Sponsorship provides increased visibility through this coverage.
